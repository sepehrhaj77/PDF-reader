Keyword Finder

Description of Project: This program take a text document (can not be a word document or anything else) and sorts all of the different words into a binary search tree. It then displays the words in order of most reoccurring in a table. The purpose of this is to highlight key words in a document that the user might need to pay attention to as it is most likely an important topic in that section of text. However, a large amount of very common, insignificant words such as "and, was, too, etc." have not been included as those are not very helpful in distinguishing important concepts.

For your convenience, you can click the words in the table to get a new tab that defines the word for you from dictionary.com!
